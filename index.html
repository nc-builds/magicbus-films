Who We Are

A film production
house that moves people.

Magic Bus Films is a Bangalore-based, founder-led production house built on the belief that great stories deserve great craft. We turn ideas, scripts, and sparks of imagination into films people remember.

We are not a content factory. We are a team that values quality over quantity, story over spectacle, and collaboration over assembly lines. From ad films to branded stories to narrative cinema, every project gets the same thing: our full attention.
